# Privacy Policy — Darsh Play

**Effective date:** May 2025
**App name:** Darsh Play
**Developer:** devgariya
**Contact:** devgariya.gariya@gmail.com

---

## Overview

Darsh Play is an educational app designed for toddlers and young children. We take the privacy of children very seriously. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- No name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information (PII)
- No photos, microphone input, camera access, or location data
- No account registration or login is required
- No in-app purchases or payment information

### Anonymous Analytics (Firebase)
Darsh Play uses **Firebase Analytics** (by Google) to collect **anonymous, non-personal** usage data, including:
- Which categories are used most (Animals, Colors, Shapes, Numbers, ABC)
- Which screens are visited and for how long
- App crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- Device type and iOS version (no device ID or advertising ID)

This data cannot be used to identify any individual user or child. It is used solely to improve app stability and content.

---

## 2. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Darsh Play is directed to children under 13. We comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)** and applicable children's privacy laws:

- We do **not** collect personal information from children
- We do **not** display third-party advertisements
- We do **not** allow in-app purchases
- We do **not** include social features, chat, or user-generated content
- Analytics data collected is **anonymous** and not tied to any individual

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Firebase Analytics (Google) | Anonymous usage analytics | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Firebase Crashlytics (Google) | Crash reporting | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |

These services may collect anonymous, aggregated device information as described in their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Anonymous analytics data is shared only with Google/Firebase as described above, under their standard terms.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase according to Google's standard retention policies. No personal data is stored by Darsh Play itself.

---

## 6. Parental Controls

Parents and guardians can control the following within the app via the **gear (⚙️) button**:

- Select learning category
- Toggle sound on/off
- Switch between Learn and Calm mode
- Return to the home screen

For additional device-level controls, Apple's **Screen Time** feature (Settings → Screen Time) allows parents to manage app usage and restrict access.
